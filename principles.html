PRINCIPLES OF ART

WHAT ARE PRINCIPLES?

This are guidelines or rules an artist fellow to produce an art work.

this principles includes;balance,propotion,unity,variety,contrast,emphasis.

1.balance

this is the equal distribution of an art work.

types of balance

symmetrical balance

this is the balance of both side in the composition,they can be same or similar.

asymmetrical balance

this is when both side are not equal or similar.

radial balance

this is when art element are arraged in a circle.

2.proportion

this is the relationship of size,shape and position of an object in a composition.

3.unity

this is the relationship of all parts in an art work.

4.variety

this is having a diferent kind of elements in an art work.

5.contrast

this is the great diferents of between two object in an art work.

6.emphasis

this is can be a drawing or painting where attracts more attantion in some cetain area in an art work.
